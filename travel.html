Summary

I have been very fortunate to have traveled quite a bit (and hope to more in the future!)
My family took my older brother and I to Europe for five weeks when I was nine but
despite the trip of a lifetime, the travel bug did not bite me until years later
when I studied abroad in Vienna, Austria in the fall of 2007. Once I graduated
in the spring of 2009 I packed my bags and moved to Zurich, Switzerland and that's
when I really took off. I lived in Zurich for a year and then spent the next two
living, working, and traveling around the globe.
